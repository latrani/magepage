From=20very past times the men that were inhabiting the Valley of the Ebro have adored to the demonstration of a concept, the cycle of the life and the death, for them the demonstration of this made is under the appearance of a Butterfly of three heads, that symbolizes change, and the cycle of the life and the death.

The capital of that zone was the city of Numancia, Numancia supported to the Cartagineses, thus the priests of many cults that when time past formed the modern Order of Hermes forced to the Roman generals to besiege the city of Numancia, 133 a.c. since th ese were knowing of the acts that were happening on Numancia, facts that they considered a perversion.

Numancia that at that time was populated by men of the ethnic of the Ilerguetes was destroyed by Roman troops in 133 AD, after a formidable site. The Numantinos could endure many days the isolation, but finally when the spirits began to weaken, and when the cannibalism was demoting them to beasts condition, preferred to accomplish a ritual (Baptism with Fire) before than been enslaved by the ones that hated Tugej.

The few men and women that survived to the site and that were expelled of their capital let in their coming generations a feeling of hatred and death that the has followed until our days. After many years they created the city of (Ilerda) Lleida.

But not all were successes, the Romans were converted to Christianity and the people of Lleida began to receive many pressures to let to enter the churches of Christ within their city. Finally ... the Tric=E1pita had to relinquish. In full Roman empire there was a Summo Sacerdo called Gelet that accomplished a series of transformations in the cult to Tugej, that they have changed forever its structure, therefore the cult that remained when the went toward Tugej, took his name, they were the Order of Gelet.=20 It is not known exactly what changes he accomplished because has not remained any witness of the cult before that date. What is known was their fled from Lleida toward the Pirineos, letting that the foreigners took the city, these entered Lleida, and the city was ransacked, violated women, dead men, destroyed churches, the Christians had been punished. After the men of Gelet, returned to a city in ruins, where soon returned to govern.

But the Christians making Gallic of their stubbornness were not expulsed and the Geletianos had to live together with them, although knowing both groups who was ordering in the city. But the enemy was near. The Church hated the Geletianos by the perversion of their acts, and the Hermetical were fearing them by been intact within their territory. After a series of incidents in full Middle Ages the Order of Hermes declared Magi War on the Order of Gelet, after serious confrontations, the Geletianos returned (not without large losses on both sides) to be defeated, therefore the Idol that it was assaulted directly must flee in hands from the then Summo Sacerdo. The Paradigm

The Oujelitas are part of the Tradition Euthanatos, but they are much more cruel that these, because they are not devoted to give the good death, they on the other hand are Necromantes in all the rule. They are from certain much more primitive point of view that the current Euthanatos.

The Oujelitas believe in their/its/your/his own god that has as demonstration a Idol, that only the Summo Sacerdo can see, and that according to the Oujelitas is a butterfly of three heads, the butterfly represents the fragility of the life, the evolution in life, the death, the destruction, THE VIOLENCE .

The Philosophy Oujelita is that of the violence, the violence carries to the man to improve, the violence brings death, and removes what is static, through the violence the oscurista can improve, furthermore through his violence returns to the other violent, what help to the ascension of his similar. when a life is lacking of sense the violence is converted into an activator of the spark, thus that person is returned violent and revives, of other form will die and relives if Tugej wants so.

The Oujelitas are not linked to fact of ascending to the rest of the humanity, on the other hand they believe that if they ascend will be able to arrive to bring the age of Tugej, that it will be the age of the death.= =20

Spheres

Correspondence: (The Vision)
Learned this field from the other Traditions, and after many years a Sacerdo adapted it to the philosophy of Tugej. They are capable of seeing through insects, of practically any nocturnal kind. In addition to already learning what is sufficient, they can leap their body in an instant and reappear where before there was an insect.

Entropy: (The Violence)
The violence is the mother of all the other spheres, through the violence is procured all, the Oujelitas can be considered corruptive by their own violence. The Oujelitas can influence violence on the live and dead beings, on inanimate and animate objects .

Forces: (Ignis)
The fire is almost the only force that the Oujelitas know, thus they are capable of creating and finishing with fire. Perhaps it is after so many years fighting with their enemies, the Flambeau.

Life: (Naturalis)
Field semirestricted between the Oujelitas, alone is given to those which are indeed linked to the Violence, because is said that if the oscurista focus in the Corpus Mutatio they will lose sight of the real reality, The Violence.

Matter: (Mud, Wax)
The water and the natural materials are what the Oujelitas know as matter, they are expert in creating mud or wax statues that at high levels imitate life.

Mind: (The impulses)
The Oujelitas are expert in awaking impulses of terror, violence, passion, etc... furthermore they can access the violent part of people and to awaken a violent spark even in the calm.

Prime: (The residue)
To see quintessence, furthermore when a Necroespiritista uses well the residues are capable of creating other impulses or violences. Thus the Necroespiritistas are capable of potent effects. The violence tends to leave a residue that a learned Oujelita th at knows this field can use.

Spirit: (Ars Mortuus)

The Oujelitas believe that the spirits are the men that someday will return to life and teach them to forge their/its/your/his own violence. Thus alone Oujelitas can accede to the inferior threshold. Furthermore, for power to use this field the Oujelitas should have something dead in hand, to act on some dead that would not be present, unless there was some connection with him. Normally and the most powerful, parts of his dead body.

Time: (The cycle)
All the Oujelitas that they begin this field, know that the life and death processes always come united in cycles, the time effects for them are united also in the cycles.=20

Current extension of the Cult

The Seed of Oujel is a cult that has been expanded a great deal during the last Three centuries. They are firmly established in the valley of the Ebro, southern France, Provenza and southern Italy. They can be connected with several cults that also carry the same stamp. So happens with Roman Necromanceres, which were outcasts from Tugej when their practices became even more terrible. The Oujelitas hate these traitors more than their hermetical enemies. In some cases, Oscuristas cults of Europe that doesn' t come from Tugej, can have with the Oujelitas a very profitable relationship.
