The Continuum

by Constantine Thomas

The Continuum is a group of mages not allied with the Technocracy or Traditions working to defend reality from the forces from the Darkness Beyond. They especially focus their work on the protection of the Shade realm of Time, Nexus, and the alternative realities which surround it.
